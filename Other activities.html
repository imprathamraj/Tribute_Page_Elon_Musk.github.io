Other activities





Elon Musk is a man of many ventures and interests beyond his major companies. He is actively involved in the Musk Foundation, where he serves as the president, supporting disaster relief efforts, research, and promoting
science and engineering education. However, the foundation has faced criticism for the relatively small amount of wealth donated compared to Musk's vast net worth. In 2013, Musk introduced the concept of the hyperloop
, a high-speed transportation system, and assigned engineers from SpaceX and Tesla to work on its development. He even organized a design competition for students to build hyperloop pods. Although it aimed to revolutionize transportation, the hyperloop project was later discontinued in 2022. Musk co-founded OpenAI, a not-for-profit AI research company, with the goal of developing artificial general intelligence that is safe and beneficial for
humanity. The company has made remarkable strides in machine learning, creating neural networks like GPT-3 and DALL-E. However, Musk had to step down from the OpenAI board to avoid potential conflicts with his role
as Tesla's CEO. In 2018, Musk became involved in a controversy surrounding the Tham Luang cave rescue in Thailand. He arranged for a mini-submarine to assist in the rescue efforts, but after a caver criticized the
submarine's utility, Musk called him a "pedo guy" on Twitter. This led to a defamation lawsuit, which Musk eventually won. During an appearance on The Joe Rogan Experience podcast, Musk made headlines by sampling
a puff of a cigar containing cannabis. This act caused controversy and had an impact on Tesla's stock price. Musk's frequent use of private jets has also drawn criticism due to the associated fossil fuel usage. In addition to his
business endeavors, Musk dabbled in music, releasing a rap track titled "RIP Harambe" and an EDM track titled "Don't Doubt Ur Vibe." These tracks garnered mixed reviews from critics. Overall, Elon Musk's involvement in
various activities showcases his diverse interests and ambitious nature. However, his actions and statements have often been a subject of polarizing views, receiving both praise and criticism from the public and media.
